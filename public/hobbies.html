Interests and Activities

I enjoy playing online computer games, where I can socialise with friends (sometimes into the early hours of the morning),
experience leadership roles and frequently work as part of a team. I also enjoy working on computers as much as I like to play
them, hardware, software or network - It's my puzzle on a rainy day.

When my eyes start to go square I like to get covered in grease working on my car/motorbike. From a minor service to replacing
the turbo, I like to keep my vehicles running smooth and reliable.

I have been a member of a Social Motorcycle Club for many years, and currently hold the office of Treasurer, I enjoy the long
rides with trusted friends and fundraising for various charities and causes.
